ABOUT US


The College of Industrial Technology (CIT) is located in Laoag City, with an external campus in Paoay, Ilocos Norte. It was chosen in 1983 as one of the 21 public technological institutes in the
Technical Vocational Education Project (TVEP) of the then Department of Education, Culture, and Sports (DECS) mandated to produce the needed manpower for industrial growth and development in the country.


ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT


The Department of Industrial Technology is committed to train students to become skilled workers, knowledgeable and competent industrial technicians, supervisors, and managers who will contribute immensely to the attainment of socio-economic stability of the region and the whole nation. The Department focuses on technical-vocational education with specializations in civil and drafting technology, computer technology, electrical technology, electronics technology, food preparation and service management, and garments technology. The Commission on Higher Education awarded the renewal of the Certificate of Program Compliance to the Department to offer the Bachelor of Science in Industrial Technology through COPC No. 31, series of 2021. Currently, the program is awarded Level III Re-accredited status by the Accrediting Agency of Chartered Colleges and Universities in the Philippines (AACCUP), Inc. The 2020 curriculum of the BSInTech was approved by the Board of Regents through the BOR Resolution No. 002-REF, series of 2020. To ensure that the competencies learned from each field of specialization are industry-relevant, the major courses are designed following the Training Regulations of TESDA under the specific competencies.
